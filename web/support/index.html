Common Questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data, posts, photos, and social connections. This action cannot be undone.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three-dot menu (...) on any post, comment, or user profile and select Report. Choose a reason and optionally add details. Our team reviews all reports.

How do I block a user?

Tap the three-dot menu (...) on any post or user profile and select Block User. Their content will be hidden from your feed and comments. You can manage blocked users in Settings > Blocked Users.

How does check-in work?

When you're at an event or activity, tap Check In & Post on the event detail page. The app verifies your location is near the venue, then opens the camera to take a photo. Your post appears in your friends' feeds.

Why does the app need my location?

Location is used only to verify you are physically at an event before checking in. Your GPS coordinates are not stored on our servers. You can browse events and the feed without granting location access.

Why does the app need camera access?

The camera is used to take photos when you check in at events. It is not used at any other time. You can revoke camera access in your device Settings.

How does "Find Friends from Contacts" work?

If you opt in, the app reads phone numbers from your contacts, hashes them on your device (SHA-256), and sends only the hashes to our server for matching. Raw phone numbers never leave your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
