Is The Museum currently open?

Yes, we're open!

Museum Open Daily, April 1 - December 31
10 AM - 5 PM (hours may vary on show days)

The Special Exhibit, Bethel Woods Grounds, and the Meet Me at Woodstock Augmented Reality Tour will reopen on May 1, 2026.

Our Golf Cart Tours will being on May 6, 2026

When do concerts start?

Great question! We have Event Gallery shows all year, and our Pavilion Concerts typically start in May. 

Where can I stay when I visit?

The Campground at Bethel Woods, open from May-October, offers Glamping, RV Sites, Tent-Only, or Car/Tent Camping.

Stay overnight just steps from the stage of a world-class concert, explore the historic site and museum, and enjoy the same views as the 1969 Woodstock Festival Campers in the beautiful Sullivan Catskill mountains.
