## Full Description

### App Store (iOS)

```
Euda helps you find things to do and go out with friends.

DISCOVER WHAT'S HAPPENING
Browse events and activities near you. Filter by category, date, price, and distance. See what's happening today, this weekend, or anytime. Weather-aware recommendations surface the right plans for the current conditions.

GO WITH FRIENDS
See which friends are going to events. RSVP to show you're interested. Find friends from your contacts and see mutual connections on every event.

CHECK IN AND SHARE
When you arrive at an event, check in with a photo. Use dual-camera mode to capture the scene and your reaction at the same time. Your friends see your posts in their feed.

EXPLORE YOUR WAY
Switch between list and map views. Use quick filters to find free activities, weekend plans, or things happening tonight. Create your own events and invite friends.

YOUR PROFILE, YOUR PLANS
Track your check-in streak and XP. See your upcoming plans and post history in one place. Customize your experience with light, dark, or system theme.

PRIVACY FIRST
Contact matching uses on-device hashing — your phone numbers never leave your device in raw form. Location is used only to show nearby events and verify check-ins. You can delete your account and all data at any time from Settings.
```

## App Review Notes

Include with every submission to help the Apple review team test the app.

```
Test Account:
Email: [INSERT TEST ACCOUNT EMAIL]
Password: [INSERT TEST ACCOUNT PASSWORD]

Notes for Reviewer:
- The app requires location permission to show nearby events and verify check-ins. You can browse events without granting location access, but check-in requires proximity to the event.
- Camera access is required only during check-in (to take a photo post). It is not used elsewhere.
- Contacts access is optional and used only on the "Find Friends from Contacts" screen (Settings > Find Friends from Contacts). Phone numbers are hashed on-device before being sent to the server.
- Account deletion is available at Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes all user data.
- The feed shows posts from accepted friends. If the test account has no friends, the feed will show an empty state.
```
